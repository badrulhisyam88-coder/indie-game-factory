# Concept: Coffee Shop Idle

**Type:** idle / incremental
**Estimated scope:** small (3-5 days)
**Status:** active

## One-line pitch
Brew coffee, serve customers, hire baristas, expand your empire one bean at a time.

## Core loop
- Click to brew coffee (manual, slow at first)
- Coffee sells automatically when customers arrive
- Buy upgrades: faster brewing, more customers, better prices
- Hire baristas to brew automatically while you click upgrades

## Progression
- 0-2 min: manual clicking, first upgrade
- 2-10 min: first auto-brewer, numbers start moving
- 10-30 min: multiple shops, prestige unlocks "franchise rep"

## Hook (the content angle)
- The "first auto-brewer" moment when the number starts climbing on its own
- Big upgrade purchase satisfaction (visible shop expansion)
- Prestige reset reveal (everything resets but you're stronger)

## Numbers to tune
- Starting coffee: 0
- First brew gives: 1 coffee
- First customer sale: 1 coffee = $1
- First upgrade cost: $10 (doubles brewing speed)
- First barista cost: $100 (brews 1 coffee/sec)
- Prestige threshold: $1,000,000

## Inspirations
- Cookie Clicker (the original)
- Adventure Capitalist (visual progression)

## Cut list
- NO custom art for v1 — use shapes + emoji
- NO sound effects for v1
- NO multiple shops — single shop, prove the loop first
- NO mobile-specific UI — desktop browser only for v1
